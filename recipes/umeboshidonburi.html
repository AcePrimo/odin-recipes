The weird and wacky world of Umoboshi Rice

This one is something special

What can you say about rice? It is what it is, a staple. If you make a good bowl of rice the best compliment you may receive is "hey man good rice" but realistically, silence is what you are looking for. A rice that has been perfectly made deserves no praise, and none is needed. It's there for you like a comfort blanket sure to pad out the rest of a lovely meal. Why would you want to mess with that? Who the fuck are you to be trying to pefect the wheel? Well I'm not suggesting that here. This isn't broke, I'm not fixing it. I'm just suggesting a way to give it some extra flair. Think of it as a nice set of rims for your car. Or some sick ass RGB for your new tower.

I am not going to lie, I don't have skill when it comes to cooking rice, but I have a really nice rice cooker so I don't have to be skilled. Should you learn how to cook rice? Sure yeah for sure, whatever- listen it's not a dying language. You don't need to feel like you are preserving some lost art, this isn't elven weaponcrafting, you aren't smithing rings of power; you're making rice- but if you feel differently, you go right for it and shine on you crazy diamond.That being said- this recipe is crazy easy and simple. There's not a lot to this but there's elegance in simplicity, and this is elegant as FUCK.

The tools of the trade

  • a rice cooker
  • a mortar and pestle
  • a small bowl
  • a rice bowl

The ingredients for success

  • 1 cup rice uncooked
  • 2 umeboshi plums
  • salt to taste
